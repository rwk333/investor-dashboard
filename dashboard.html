Strategy & Forward Look

We have a clear, focused plan to capitalize on our current momentum, optimize for profitability, and scale the business for long-term success.

Health First Brand Repositioning

We are sharpening our brand messaging to lead with our strongest assets: superior health and taste credentials. This aligns with consumer demand and retailer priorities, moving beyond food waste as the primary message while retaining it as a core value.

COGS & Margin Enhancement

A full supply chain review is underway to protect profitability against retailer margin pressure. We are working closely with our manufacturer to optimize cost of goods, ensuring we can scale effectively without compromising on quality.

Team Structure for Scale

We are building a team of specialists focused on commercial delivery, activation, and operations. This ensures we have the right expertise to successfully execute our largest launches in H2 2025 and beyond.

Minecraft Deal Status

Terms have been significantly improved (70% reduction in MGV). However, we are strategically reviewing this as the optimal use of marketing budget, with our new retail partner preferring a focus on the core brand first. We plan to revisit this in 2026.

The Ask: Fueling Our Growth

As planned, we are kicking off a larger fundraising round in H2 2025. This investment is crucial to:

  • Fund the successful rollout of our major new listings.
  • Expand the team with key specialist talent.
  • Support new product development to broaden our appeal.
  • Maintain strong market momentum and drive brand awareness.

Introductions to new potential investors aligned with our health-led mission would be massively appreciated.
